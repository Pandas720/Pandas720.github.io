  • Chappie was filmed in Johannesburg, South Africa.
  • The character of Chappie was originally going to be voiced by Sharlto Copley using a South African accent, but the filmmakers decided to have him use an American accent instead.
  • Childhood Home: The house used as Hippo’s lair in the movie belongs to director Neill Blomkamp.
  • Chappie’s design was inspired by a combination of different robots from science fiction, including WALL-E and R2-D2.
